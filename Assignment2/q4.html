Destination: Paris

Paris, the city of light, is known for its iconic Eiffel Tower, exquisite cuisine, and rich culture.

  • Tip 1: Visit the Eiffel Tower at night for a stunning view.
  • Tip 2: Try local pastries like croissants and macarons.
  • Tip 3: Use public transportation to explore the city efficiently.
